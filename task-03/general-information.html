General Information

All races take place on Saturdays, rain or shine unless conditions pose a safety risk.

Event Timetable

  • Gates open: 07:30 AM
  • Mandatory rider briefing: 08:30 AM
  • First rider on course: 09:00 AM
  • Final heat concludes: ~04:00 PM
  • Gates close: 05:00 PM

Additional Information

  • The race director reserves the right to adjust schedules or cancel events due to extreme weather, trail conditions, or other safety concerns.
  • Schedule changes will be communicated via official email, SMS alerts, and posted to the race website at least 24 hours prior to the event.
